We take pride in offering a wide genre of books, including children's, young adult, science fiction, fantasy, mystery, horror, romance, classics, vintage books, and non-fiction. We are also proud to support our community by offering a selection of both new and used books written by Idaho authors.

fun for the whole family!games & puzzles

We offer both new and used puzzles. They range from easy to not-so-easy to oh-dear, what have I gotten myself into! Our used puzzles are guaranteed to have all their pieces. We also carry a wide variety of new and used board and card games. Our game and puzzle stock varies, and we may not have duplicate items to offer if something sells out, but you can follow us on Facebook to be among the first to see our newly acquired games and puzzles. If something catches your eye you can call, text, Facebook message, or email us to purchase it!
