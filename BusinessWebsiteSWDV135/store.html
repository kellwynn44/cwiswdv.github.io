Proudly serving caldwell  since 2011 About Our Store

Founded in 2011 by co-owners Amy Perry and Ken Nelson, The Rubaiyat LLC is located in the heart of downtown Caldwell, ID. A recent change of location allowed Amy and Ken to expand their selection of books, including a new section  dedicated to Idaho field and hiking guides. The store's new location also gave them the opportunity to host in-store events such as book readings, author signings, book clubs, writing groups, crafts, and art classes.

We hope that you will fall in love with our whimsical store, and invite you to join us for one of our fun community events!
